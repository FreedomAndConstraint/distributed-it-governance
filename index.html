Decentralized IT governance

Decentralized IT governance

During 2015 mini-workshop at GBI we have started to review a broad family of emerging Internet technologies which we called distributed IT governance systems. The goal of such review was to come up with a conceptual perspective to the emerging technological space. This gitbook is such conceptual perspective -- we propose to frame the technology space using concepts of synchrony, asynchrony, centralized and distributed systems, emerging consensus and relations between them.

Please note, that this document is a work in progress, therefore may contain inconsistencies, imprecise or even wrong usage of concepts or terms. We hope to eventually correct them. Any comments, criticisms, thoughts, etc. would be of great help. Please add them using Hypothes.is annotation system provided here.
